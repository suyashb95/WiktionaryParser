Serbo-Croatian[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From hd (walk, pace). Cognates include Czech chodit and Russian (xodit).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /xdati/
  • Hyphenation: hodati

Verb[edit]

hdati impf (Cyrillic spelling )

  1. (intransitive) to walk, pace
  2. (intransitive) to stride
  3. (intransitive) to saunter, stroll, amble
  4. (intransitive, figuratively, colloquial) to date (to be romantically involved with another)
  5. (intransitive, figuratively, colloquial) run, work, function (operate or fulfill its purpose satisfactorily)
