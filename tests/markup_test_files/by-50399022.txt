====Usage notes====
* In formal language, it is used for the the [[third person]] of the infinitive {{m|cs|být}}, both [[singular]] and [[plural]] and with the [[second person]] of the verb {{l|cs|být}}.
*: {{ux|cs|Zahrál '''by''' [[sis]] jednu hru?|translation=Would you [like to] play one game?}}
* In colloquial Czech, ''by'' is commonly used in place of other conditional forms of ''být'' in both singular and plural. That is, ''by'' is a shared shorthand for {{m|cs|bych}}, {{m|cs|bys}}, {{m|cs|bychom}}, {{m|cs|bysme}} and {{m|cs|byste}}.
