Danish

Etymology

From Latin concursus, from concurrere (“to run together, agree”).

Pronunciation

  • (deprecated use of |lang= parameter) IPA(key): /kɔnɡkurs/, [kʰʌŋˈkʰuɐ̯ˀs]

Adjective

konkurs (indeclinable)

  1. bankrupt
  2. insolvent

Synonyms

  • bankerot Template:pos a
  • fallit Template:pos a

Noun

konkurs c (singular definite konkursen, plural indefinite konkurser)

  1. failure
  2. bankruptcy
  3. liquidation
