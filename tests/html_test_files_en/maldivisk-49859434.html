Danish[edit]

Adjective[edit]

maldivisk

  1. Maldivian

Inflection[edit]

Inflection of maldivisk
Positive Comparative Superlative
Common singular maldivisk — —2
Neuter singular maldivisk — —2
Plural maldiviske — —2
Definite attributive1 maldiviske — —
1) When an adjective is applied predicatively to something definite, the corresponding "indefinite" form is used.
2) The "indefinite" superlatives may not be used attributively.

Norwegian Bokmål[edit]

Adjective[edit]

maldivisk (neuter singular maldivisk, definite singular and plural maldiviske)

  1. Maldivian (relating to the Maldives and Maldivians)

Noun[edit]

  • maldivisk m (definite singular maldivisken, uncountable)
  1. Maldivian or Dhivehi (language)

Synonyms[edit]

  • (language) dhivehi

Related terms[edit]

  • Maldivene
  • maldiver

References[edit]
