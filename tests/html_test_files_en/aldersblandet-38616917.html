Norwegian Bokmål

Etymology

From alder +‎ -s- +‎ blandet (past participle of blande).

Adjective

aldersblandet (neuter singular aldersblandet, definite singular and plural aldersblandede or aldersblandete)

  1. mixed-age (attributive), of mixed age
